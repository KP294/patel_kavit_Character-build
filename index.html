Indira Gandhi

Indira Gandhi was India's third prime minister, serving from 1966 until 1984, when her life ended in assassination. She was the daughter of Jawaharlal Nehru, India's first prime minister. The lone child of Jawaharlal Nehru, India's first prime minister, Indira Gandhi ascended to the position after his death in the mid-1960s.
